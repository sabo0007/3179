Field of Education (All Students)

EFTSL by broad field; bar colour shows growth vs 2023 (darker = stronger growth).

Key takeaways

  • Information Technology and Health carry some of the largest loads.
  • Education shows notable growth vs 2023, reflected by darker bars.
  • Creative Arts and Architecture remain smaller in overall load.

Key takeaways

  • Attainment rises sharply from ages 20–24 to early 30s, then gradually plateaus.
  • Gender gap is small overall; the largest gap appears in the 25–29 cohort.
  • By 55–59, the gap narrows again — cohorts converge.
